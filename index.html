You may need to re-open your browser for privacy settings to take effect.

What does this magical switch do?

Ad and Tracker Blocking

Raaz will block most intrusive and annoying ads and trackers from websites. Some ads can be malicious and trackers can be used to collect data about your browsing behaviour.

Third-Party Cookie Blocking

Fret not Cookie Monster, These are not the cookies you eat! Much like trackers, cookies, especially third-party cookies, can be used to collect data about you and send you targeted ads.

Referer header Blocking

Referer headers tell the current website where you are coming from. This can be used to track your browsing behaviour or stealing your information.

This will remind you about safe email and anti-phishing practices everytime you open your emails.
